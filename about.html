About Campus Cribs

Your trusted partner for student housing at FUTA

Our Story

Founded with a mission to solve the housing challenges faced by FUTA students, Campus Cribs has become the most trusted platform for student accommodation.

We understand the struggles of finding safe, affordable, and convenient housing near campus. That's why we've created a platform that connects students with verified landlords and properties, eliminating scams and ensuring a smooth housing search experience.

Since our inception, we've helped thousands of students find their perfect home away from home, building a community based on trust, transparency, and reliability.

Our Mission

To provide FUTA students with a secure, transparent, and efficient platform for finding verified housing options, eliminating scams and ensuring peace of mind throughout the accommodation search process.

Our Vision

To become the leading student housing platform across Nigerian universities, setting the standard for reliability, transparency, and student-focused service in the off-campus accommodation industry.
